About

Some people think that happiness comes from one direction; study hard -> get degree -> get a job -> get married -> have kids -> boom you're happy. Unfortunately, that's not the case. Many people have done that already and they are not happy. School teaches one how to make living, but it doesn't teach how to make a life, it doesn't help you how to be happy and healthy. Because happy and healthy doesn't consider education. This training is designed by youth for youth, to share technical experience along with life experience in order to live happy and healthy life.
